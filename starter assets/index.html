Who We Are

Bharath University is a semi-residential university college in South India created under the Chennai State Private University Act 2005 and recognized by UGC under Section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956.

Bharath University offers around 200 programmes and courses at diploma, undergraduate, graduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels. The university has more than 30,000 students graduating every year
